A really really cool product that quickly adjusts your water habit. My fiancee and I both got one, and it turned the healthy habit of hydration into a game, much like Fitbit or other health data tracking. Even though it updates the app automatically, whether or not it's open on your phone, I always get sucked into opening the app after every sip just to watch it go up! Such a cool piece of technology, excited to see what they'll do in the future with app updates and the like. If you enjoy data tracking with a healthy spin-- don't wait, get this product!
