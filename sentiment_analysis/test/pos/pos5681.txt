In order to use this with the OnePlus 3 you must do the following. Insert microSD into meenova, and then insert the meenova into your device. Next go to Settings --> Storage & USB and in the upper right of the screen tap on the menu button (3 dots) and select Enable OTG. If your device does not automatically find the meenova card reader back out of Storage & USB and re-enter and the OnePlus 3 will recognize the card reader and display its contents.In order to transfer files simply use the stock Files app or whichever one you prefer. To remove the meenova card reader go back to Storage & USB select the menu button in the upper right hand corner and select Disable OTG.If this helped you out please tap the button below.
