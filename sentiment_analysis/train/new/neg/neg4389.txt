I want to love this. I really do. We have the basic set and wanted to expand upon it. Well. We can expand, but only one or two ways. There are very little options when it comes to configuring this. I even wrote to the company asking if they sold extra parts and explained my frustration. I received a very succinct email stating they do not sell parts separately. Their loss. I would have bought a bunch as this is a toy that could be great for my little one until she is at least 3 years. As it is, we'll probably pass it along to someone else, practice keeping marbles out of our mouths and move on to Marbleworks. It does take up a fair amount of storage space, which is another con.
