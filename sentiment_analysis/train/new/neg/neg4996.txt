I was so in love with this pattern when I found it for our sons room. I ordered these decals to add to his room. However when the package arrived I was not in love with this product. The decals did not stick on the wall. Some of the decals had a crease in them from packaging. I would not recommend spending the money on these. Although they are a nice decorative addition to a room they fall off constantly and you spend more time picking them up rather than enjoying them
