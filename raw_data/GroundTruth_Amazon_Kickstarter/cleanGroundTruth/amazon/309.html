These stainless steel coasters with a built-in bottle opener are great conversation starters.




Brushed stainless steel for durability.

These FuhlSpeed Coaster Craft stainless steel coasters are actually two-in-one handy party tools. They're made of durable stainless steel and designed to last a lifetime. Fun graphics appeal to beer lovers.

Product description

If you haven't spent much time thinking about coasters, then you're probably in the majority. We were the same way until we decided to make some coasters that are functional and aesthetically pleasing. The Coaster craft 6-pack offers 6 stainless steel coasters with fun craft beer graphics and sayings that will make you wish you had thought of them. And since a Coaster with only one function isn't too exciting, Coaster craft coasters have a built in bottle opener too! you no longer have to let your beer or soda sit and wait while you frantically search for a bottle opener. Bring these coasters with you when visiting friends or family. Let the coasters worry about the condensation from your bottle and enjoy not getting yelled at for damaging the countertop or table.
