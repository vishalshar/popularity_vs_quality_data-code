5.0 out of 5 starsPerfect
ByRyanon June 10, 2016
Verified Purchase
I've been doing sous vide cooking for a long time and I do a lot of it. I built my own cooker about 5 years ago that I was pretty proud of. I swore I'd never buy a commercial unit, but eventually I caved because I wanted to cook some things requiring hotter temperatures that would have melted the fountain pump I was using for circulation. This thing went on sale and I never looked back. The setup is so simple. The clamp works with pretty much any container and holds securely. The interface is super simple. Just roll the scroll wheel to dial in the temperature and touch the start button. It heats up quickly and has very powerful circulation. It maintains temperature within 0.1 degrees. The wifi/bluetooth is cool, but it's really been more a novelty for me. I guess the idea is to be able to start it up remotely and have your food ready when you get home or whatever, but I'm not convinced it's safe to leave food sitting in a tepid pool of water for hours. Especially with meat, you either want it refrigerator cold or at cooking temperature. I don't think it's a good idea to let it linger in between for very long. Still, it's kinda cool to check in on it and be able to control it from your phone. The build quality is very solid. It's easy to take apart and clean, not that it requires much cleaning. I usually just wipe it dry. I've been using it about 3-5 times a week for a few months now and haven't had one hiccup. I've done multiple 24+ hour cooks and have done a few cooks maintaining 5 gallons of water at 176 F. I don't have one single complaint.

5.0 out of 5 starsGreat hardware, software much improved
ByDoug Won February 28, 2016
Verified Purchase
As people say, there are two aspects to this - the actual unit and the Wifi/app part. The actual unit does what it says - it keeps the water circulating and at a certain temperature. I've made steaks, chicken, a tri tip, and a pork belly, and no surprises. The results are very nice and the temperature is extremely stable.

The app is a moving target. I've had this unit only a couple of weeks and have had three versions of the iPhone app. Which is great because they've fixed most of the problems people are complaining about in the other reviews, and bad because I have to reconnect the WiFi each time they do that and it always takes 2-3 tries.

As of this writing you can do a few things with the app. You can see the temperature. You can set the temp and start the unit. I've actually used this to pre-heat the water so it was already hot by the time I got home. It's actually talking to a server somewhere so you can do this out in the world - your phone doesn't have to be on Wifi, it just needs to be on the Internet. There's also a timer although I just use the regular phone timer.

They just in the last day or two added features to help manage actually leaving the food in the bath all day and starting cooking at work so it's done when you get home without dying horribly from all the bacteria growing in the meantime. They have instructions (frozen food and lots of ice) and you can set it up to notify you if the temperature enters the bacteria danger zone. In that case you would probably just start cooking the food a bit early - there's usually a pretty big window in cooking sous vide style so it shouldn't be any big deal. This appears to be a fairly workable approach but I'd try it a few times with cheaper meat to make sure it's 100% reliable and to know how long your ice lasts before sticking in your prime grade ribeye, you know?
