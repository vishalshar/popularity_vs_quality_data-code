4.0 out of 5 starsNeeds a lot of break-in!
ByAmazon Customeron June 6, 2016
Color: Green|Verified Purchase
My first impressions were pretty poor as it was not broken in. Turning the dial to all the presta valve spots and oscillating the ring made it much easier to take on and off. It's also much easier if you only open presta valves slightly instead of all the way.

It's also worth noting that to do it one handed the tire needs to be turned such that the valve is on the bottom so you have something to push against. This can be a problem for tubeless users as inflating the tire in this orientation can clog the valve with sealant, as well as shoot sealant everywhere while putting it on...

The pump head is expensive and needs a lot of break-in, but it's easy to use and is much easier on presta valve cores than lever-type heads.

4.0 out of 5 starsOnce broken in, works perfectly and quickly.
ByStephan Krauseon August 3, 2016
Color: Green
At first I was bit frustrated with the pump head, as I found it difficult to put on or pull off (I only use the presta option). A lot of air would escape when I tried to push it on. Or, a lot of air would come out if I did not pull it off fast enough (or on, really). I have patience, guessed the pump-head needed time to break-in and now works as advertised. I am very happy with it. Once broken in, little to no air escapes (outside of what happens with most pumps) and the one handed operation works very well.

As for the build quality, it is built very well and I don't see any of the plastic bits breaking anytime soon. The people behind the product are true engineering nerds (and REALLY nice people) and feel they did their homework. The only issue I have with the pump, is the lettering on the ring. Even with my 20/20 vision, it is difficult to make out the P or S markings. This is not unique to the product however, most products I have owned have the black on black writing (white on white, green on green..etc). On the next revision, it would be nice to have a contrasting color.

If you have the patience to get through the break-in period, I wholly recommend the product. Even if you don't have the patience, I would still purchase one if your current pump-head is going south. This is simply the best replacement option out there.
