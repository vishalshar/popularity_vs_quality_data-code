The FuhlSpeed Popcorn Ball makes perfectly popped and seasoned popcorn every time.




Pop, season, and serve in one bowl.

The FuhlSpeed Popcorn Ball Microwavable Popcorn Maker and Mixer is a revolutionary kitchen accessory that's designed to pop, season, and serve delicious popcorn from one container. It features a hinged lid that seals corn in while popping and conveniently flips open when adding seasoning, and butter. After popping, add the seasoning, close the lid and spin. It's that easy!

Designed to make seasoning easier.

The innovative shape of the FuhlSpeed Popcorn Ball Microwavable Popcorn Maker and Mixer is the secret to it's success. The round shape and hinged lid make it easier than ever to add seasoning so that every kernel gets coated with flavor. The bottom features a proprietary design that ensures toppings and seasonings go into the mix rather than clinging to the sides.

Ideal for other snacks and treats.

Besides popping corn, the FuhlSpeed Popcorn Ball Microwavable Popcorn Maker and Mixer is a perfect mixer for other treats, even salads. After spinning the ingredients together, snap it apart for serving. The flat base and removable top makes it perfect for serving salads, chips, trail mix, cereal treats, candy, and other dry snacks. Dishwasher safe makes clean up a breeze.

Product description

Are you interested in perfectly seasoned popcorn every time how about eliminating the process of making popcorn in a bag that becomes super hot and then pouring it into a bowl with this microwavable popcorn Ball, you can place your popcorn kernels directly inside and microwave until they are all popped. After you take it out of the microwave, all you have to do is open the lid and pour in whatever toppings you'd like. Then, close the lid and spin the Ball using the two finger divots on the sides of the Ball. Open up the top of the Ball and enjoy perfectly distributed ingredients. While you're not eating the popcorn, have fun rolling the Ball around and knowing that your butter, seasoning, or other favorite toppings are constantly covering every kernel. If popcorn isn't your thing, toss some trail mix or salad in the Ball and use it the same way to mix all your ingredients. The bottom of the Ball even has a turbulator to ensure the toppings go all the way through the mix rather than staying on the sides. Now, get creative and mix up some great snacks either for yourself or for the family

3.0 out of 5 starsOnly Good For Pre-Popped Popcorn
ByGeoffrey KleinmanVINE VOICEon August 19, 2016
Verified Purchase
On paper, this is a very cool gadget, a popcorn ball which allows you to pop and then evenly distribute toppings to all the popcorn. Of the two jobs it's designed to do, The Popcorn Ball does best with distributing toppings, especially for pre-popped popcorn. Place popped corn in The Popcorn Ball and a topping and then spin. It's fun to toss around this popcorn ball and since it seals with a silicone gasket, the toppings don't leak and the lid stays well shut when mixing.

As a microwave popper, The Popcorn Ball really falls short. First off, there are NO instructions in or on the box, so you have to go searching to find just how much popcorn to put into The Popcorn Ball. I had to do a ton of searching online to find it which is: " 1/2 cup or less of dry popping corn for 2 to 2 and a half minutes on high. Do not use oils or butter during the microwaving." We followed this exactly and got a mixture of unpopped kernels and burnt corn. Since this is billed as a Microwavable Popcorn popper, this poor showing was enough to knock two stars off the rating.

The lack of recipes (either on the box, in an instruction booklet or online) really hampers this product. The brand has a Facebook page and kickstarter page which show off what you can do with the popcorn ball, but no instructions or recipes on how! It's frustrating to be shown the possibilities of a product and then not given the needed info to make that happen.

The Popcorn Ball is a fun little gadget, and if you realize that you need to use something else to pop your popcorn, you'll enjoy it a lot more. I hope the company will spend the time and resources to include an instructions and recipe booklet along with this popcorn ball, it's a real omission.

Note, this is the same Popcorn Ball that was done as a Kickstarter campaign, I'm not sure why it's listed so strange here at amazon and not just listed as "The Popcorn Ball"
