Product description

Workout Smarter

Smart Rope is an LED-embedded jump rope that displays your fitness data in mid-air as you work out. Smart Rope syncs with our mobile fitness platform, Smart Gym, to track your jump count, calories burned, and workout times. Smart Gym will also recommend interval training sessions for you based on your skill level, and keeps you motivated by unlocking awards and allowing you to compete with your friends via Facebook.

Fitness Data You'll Actually Use

Unlike activity trackers or wearables, Smart Rope uses the mechanics of your workout to create accurate, relevant data you'll actually use to improve. Magnetic sensors in the handle track each full revolution of the rope as one jump, instead of monitoring generic motion.
Smart, Fun, Immersive

As a standalone device, Smart Rope creates a fun, addictive fitness experience that keeps you going. Paired with our Smart Gym mobile platform, you'll stay motivated as you train with our recommended interval sessions, unlock awards, and compete with your friends.

  • Syncs with the Smart Gym mobile platform, available for iOS, Android, Apple watch, and pebble watch - monitor progress and compete with friends
  • Charge in 2 hours for 36 hours of activity or about 30 days of use

3.0 out of 5 starsGreat...in theory
ByJacquie Floydon June 17, 2016
This rope IS super cool. Unfortunately, I am on rope number 4 and the lights haven't lasted for longer than a week. Customer service has been more than happy to exchange my rope for me each time, and on rope 4 they even offered to test it for me before sending it out. Problem being, they tested it for a week, they always broke on me on week 2, so this rope only lasted one week before the lights stopped working. I finally gave up and just asked them to let me know when they have a newer version ready. I can't keep reboxing my rope and driving out to FedEx (and I hate my neighborhood FedEx, they are impossible to deal with!).
I still use it every day (30-40 minutes a day, jumping on a laminate floor) because the counter and stats still work. I just have to leave the app open on my phone nearby to see the count and make sure the counting software doesn't freeze up, which it does sometimes, too. When the lights were working, you could tell it stopped counting when the lights went out. Then you just used a paperclip on the reset button and started jumping again. This time I have to keep the phone on a shelf nearby and watch the app as it counts the jumps and make sure it doesn't stop.
If you are buying it for the lights, don't bother. If I couldn't get it to work after 4 rope exchanges, I can't imagine it works for anyone else either. 4 is a bit excessive to me. I really would love to have a rope with lights that work. It was cool while it lasted.
If you want just the counter and the stats, it's a fine tool.
Fix the lights smart rope and I will definitely tout this rope on the highest mountains! Until then, kind of disappointing. Especially at this price.
