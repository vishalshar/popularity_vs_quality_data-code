About the product
  • The Most Comfortable & Convenient Smartphone Sports Armband. Low contact with skin reduces sweat.
  • Patented dual-stage locking mechanism ensures your phone stays securely on your arm
  • Ideal for tracking your fitness via apps like Strava, easy access to music, and never missing a phone call or text message again
  • Fully adjustable nylon lycra armband will fit any arm size with circumference 17-38cm or 6.5-15 inches
  • Compatible with all Quad Lock Cases & Adaptors
More
Offer Type:Sports Armband ONLY

The Quad Lock Sports Armband introduces the convenience of the Quad Lock Mounting System to runners, joggers and gym goers. No longer will you have to struggle trying to fit your smartphone into a neoprene or plastic pouch. With the Quad Lock Sports Armband you are only a click away from easy access to your smartphone and can be confident that it is securely attached to your arm. Never miss a phone call or text message again. The band is made from a comfortable Nylon Lycra that can be adjusted for both small and large arms between a circumference of 17 - 38cm or 6.5 - 15 inches. Compatible with all Quad Lock Cases and Adapters. Quad Lock Case not included.

Been using for about three weeks. I've used many iphone armbands in the past and without a doubt this is the most solid and well built I have ever seen or used.

I love the open design and that I can keep the case on all the time (tho I do occasionally get asked about it). The case seems to offer decent protection and while not stylish, isn't ugly either.

The band is a clever design with two areas to tighten, one on each side of the phone. I used it for a half marathon and it stayed in place, was comfortable and didn't chaff (I put it over my sleeve).

I really like the case and will continue to use. So why only 4 stars and not 5;

-Cost. It is expensive. It is very well made, unique and works well. But it is a lot of money. Was thinking of getting the car mount, but another $50-60? Bit much. I'll keep using my $10 universal vent mount.
-Weight. The case doesn't add much in terms of size, but does in weight. It is what I would call a heavier case. It feels solid to the touch, but it adds "a lot" of weight to the light phone.
-The arm band works great for running, but not quite as well for lifting. There is not as much stretch as in some other bands I've used. This makes it feel a bit snug when lifting. Usually I lossen it a bit when lifting and seems to solve the issue. I'd like a bit more flex in the band. But then that might not work with the heavy case while running.

It it were $20 less, just a bit lighter and just a little easier to stretch the arm band, I'd give 10 stars ;). As is still think an outstanding case. And recommend.

4.0 out of 5 starsQuad Lock is the best thing for active people since... ever.
ByFrankon April 7, 2016
Offer Type: Sports Armband ONLY|Verified Purchase
Quad Lock is a company that came up with the most fantastic idea. One mounting system across all your platforms. It's just incredible.

I've used Quad Lock's armband for several months now (twice a day, 5-6 days a week) for any of my general physical activities. I wear it to the gym and on runs. I have the QL bike mount, so that's not a necessity for me... needless to say, I love this company and the products that they offer... so why only 4 out of 5 stars?

It's the velcro. The band is wonderful and fits even larger sized arms, but the velcro wears out over time. You can make certain adjustments so the velcro fits on different parts of the band, but it just wears out over time. I hate having to spend thirty dollars every time I need to replace the armband, but simply by the elegant nature of this product, it's still worth it.

If QL fixes the velcro issues in a future version of this product, it would easily catapult it to 5/5. I currently own and use three different Quad Lock mounts (armband, bike mount, and car mount) and I think they're wonderful. The best part is you're not limited to using their cases if you don't like--you can get a universal adapter (which is the route I have taken. I put it on my Lifeproof case). This lets you change phones with ease of mind, knowing you don't have to get a new mount every single time you change a phone! All you need is a new universal adapter, which is way cheaper than replacing many different components.
