Product description

Simply Gum launched in 2013 as the only all natural, premium chewing gum brand on the market. We started the company because we couldn't find a truly natural and great tasting gum in the market.

It turns out that the regular chewing gum you see on grocery store shelves is filled with artificial substances, including some of the same components used in the manufacture of car tires, plastic bottles, and white glue, as well as artificial sweeteners like aspartame. Consumers are kept in the dark about what they are chewing because the FDA allows conventional gum brands to hide up to 80 synthetic ingredients in the catch-all term "gum base" on the label.

We knew we could do better.

In New York, our gum chefs craft the best ingredients into the highest quality gum on the market. The result is a lightly sweetened, totally natural gum that’s better for your body and the environment.

 

 

Simply Gum makes all natural premium chewing gum, without any plastic, synthetics, or artificial flavors.  The result is a lightly sweetened, totally natural gum that's better tasting, better for your body, and better for the environment. If you love ginger candy or ginger chews, you'll love our ginger gum!
Because we only use natural ingredients, our gum is not only better for your body, it's also biodegradable and better for the planet.  Our innovative packaging includes post-chew wrappers to make it easy to dispose of when complete. Each order contains 6 packs with 15 pieces each, for a total of 90 pieces of gum.
Regular chewing gum is packed with artificial substances that are potentially toxic, including plastic, aspartame, and BHT. Unlike conventional gum brands, Simply Gum uses all natural ingredients without ANY synthetics.

4.0 out of 5 starsGood gum for the right person.
ByC. Lawsonon December 23, 2016
Verified Purchase
As others have stated, the gum is packaged loosely in a little white box. The company put effort into packaging and design, so it looks nice as a little gift.

They don't rattle, which is nice if you keep it in a pocket. The box isn't very thick, so it wouldn't stand up to much resistance if you put it in pockets of snugly fitting jeans.

The nibs are irregular, about the size of a pea, and seem to have a light coating of a floury material that I assume is to prevent sticking. I would say two nibs approximately equates to a traditional piece of gum.

Definitely tastes like ginger, but doesn't pack a spicy punch. The flavor wears after about 45 minutes for me, which I don't mind especially for a natural product.

I wanted to love it, but the deal breaker for me is that I can't easily just spit it into a trash can when I'm done with a piece. I keep a trash can at my work desk, so I didn't expect to need the included disposal papers. When I attempt to spit out the gum, about half the time it catches in my teeth and turns into a long string that's dangling from my mouth. I'm a healthcare provider, so I spit out my gum any time I need to grab a patient. For me, that added hassle multiple times a day means I'll be passing off the rest of my case to friends and family to try.

If you don't mind signing up for a project to get the gum out of your mouth, I'd say give it a go. For me, it'd be a five star product if it could be spit it just a bit more easily (bonus points if it were a bit spicier).

4.0 out of 5 starsI like their gum, but I'm not a fan of their ginger
ByJoe WoodTOP 1000 REVIEWERon May 30, 2015
I was a backer of Simply Gum's Kickstarter efforts a few years ago, so I've been a customer for some time. I started with the cinnamon flavor but now have tried them all.

Personally, I love the licorice flavor the best, but none of them are bad. That being said, the ginger isn't my favorite. I like ginger, but it seems I don't like ginger gum. If you *do* like ginger, you'll probably like this. As with all their gum, it's a high-quality product... it's just that this flavor isn't for me.

I readily admit that the gum is expensive - really expensive, for the quantity you get. However, first of all, it's all natural, which I realize doesn't usually mean a whole lot, but this company really does make their gum from scratch with high quality ingredients. It is obvious when you chew it that it's not like the gum you normally get. It's softer, it lasts a lot longer without stiffening up, and, although the flavor fades, there's never a bad taste that follows (or at least an artificial taste?) as you'd get with most gum.

Try at least one of their flavors - whichever sounds appealing to you. I truly think you'll like it, even if you don't make it your go-to gum. It'll at least make it to an occasional treat, mainly because it's different in a good way.
