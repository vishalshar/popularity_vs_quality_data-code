Every aspect of this bag screams low rent garbage, do not waste your money. It's painfully obvious why they couldn't get a deal on Shark Tank, this thing is a piece of trash!

2.0 out of 5 starsPoor Quality - Falling Apart When it Arrived
ByTorraine A; Williamson February 28, 2017
Color: Brown|Verified Purchase
Quite disappointed with this purchase. When it arrived in the mail, it was already falling apart. As you can see from the image, when I first opened it, the metal "hole" for the latch that keeps the main compartment closed was already missing a screw. I contacted their support. Their solution is to send me another screw and instruct me to superglue it in place. In addition to that, some of the elastic on parts of the bag don't seem to be very durable. It looks like after a little real use, it will just wear out and not return to its original position.
So the size of the bag is about what I expected. There are a decent number of pockets/compartments etc on the bag. Some are well-placed and thought out. For example, I really like the bottle holder pocket that straps shut when not in use and the pocket on the shoulder strap. It is not big enough to comfortably fit the really big smart phones like an iPhone Plus. But it is great for medium to smaller size devices. And the elastic holds them firmly in place in a very convenient location. I do hope the elastic is better quality than the latch though. Otherwise, when it wears, that will be very inconvenient.
In summary, the bag is well designed, but not very well built. Perhaps 3.0 will include a higher quality version. Depending on how long this bag lasts, I will be needing a replacement soon.
