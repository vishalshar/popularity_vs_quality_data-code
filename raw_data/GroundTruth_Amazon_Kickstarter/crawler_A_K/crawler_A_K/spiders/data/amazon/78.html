About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Comfortable Functional Versatile!

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

Convenience on the go. That is what Yoganshi Shah thought of when her husband, Hiral Sanghavi, kept forgetting his neck pillow when he travelled. Initially together they thought maybe an app would work to keep a working packing list. Yoganshi had a brilliant idea of creating a jacket that essentially embodies “everything has a place, and everything in its place.” The outcome was a functional jacket designed for accessibility and comfort. The BauBax jacket has 15 features that helps all travelers and people on-the-go stay organized with pockets for a tablet, smart phones, sunglasses, and even a drink pocket. To add to the overall functionality, there is space to hold earphones, built-in eye mask, gloves that extend from the sleeves, and comes with a microfiber cloth to keep all eyewear sparkly clear. Of course it has the built-in neck pillow that will not be left at home again.

What makes your product special?

You likely never heard of a travel jacket before Baubax, but it's the jacket you always needed but never existed.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

It is good to have clothing that solves problems you face in your day-to-day life. We are happy that Baubax is doing that for tens of thousands of customers worldwide.

ByAmazon Customeron January 13, 2017

I found the Baubax travel sweatshirt to be useful yet burdensome. My favorite aspects of the sweatshirt were the soft and comfortable fabric used to make the jacket and the elongated hood (the eye mask) that covers the face just right for sleeping purposes. The pocket warmers and gloves also kept my hands warm and comfy. In terms of burdensome, I had issues with several of the sweatshirt's features (to the point where I'm considering to return it). The detachable stylus easily fell off from the zipper and I had difficulties removing the pen from the stylus's cap. The passport, sunglasses, and iPad pockets noticeably made the sweatshirt bulkier and angular, rather than forming naturally over those objects and keeping them hidden. The neck pillow (which was placed within the hood) not only made the hood heavier but also uncomfortable for my head. Lastly, other than the 'unformfitting' pockets, my other main issue was the heavy weight of the whole sweatshirt; as someone who travels often, I can see this being an issue in the future when trying to move quickly through airports. Overall, I found this product to be overpriced; a range between $60 and $100 would be more reasonable (even if these issues were fixed). *Another unhappy side note: my sweatshirt was shipped with a giant rip in the iPhone pocket; not very durable if you ask me :/
