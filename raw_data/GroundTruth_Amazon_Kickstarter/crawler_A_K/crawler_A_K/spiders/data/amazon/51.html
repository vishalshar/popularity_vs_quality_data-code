Omnivore was founded in 2013 by Angelo Garro, Veronica Ronchi and Beth Malik, with the encouragement and support of Alice Waters, Michael Pollan, and many other local chefs.

We create nutrient-packed cooking products, only crafted in small batches to ensure the best flavor and the highest quality. Our ingredients are 100% organic, and when possible, locally sourced—we are inspired by our grandmothers' sensitivity and respect for nature's bounty, not by food trends.

Omnivore Salt

“This salt is so indispensable. I bring a bag everywhere I go!” Alice Waters

Omnivore Salt is a subtle and original blend of locally harvested sea salt, and organic spices, made by the Italian master of ironwork and slow food icon Angelo Garro. This versatile mix, will enhance the natural flavors of your ingredients, and is easy to use across any type of cuisine.

Omnivore Salt will revolutionize your cooking, and will become a stample in your pantry!

Meet Omnivore Salt




Omnivore salt is a family recipe taught by Angelo’s grandmother Sebastiana, while he was a young boy in Sicily.

Omnivore is committed to using as many locally sourced, sustainable and fair trade certified ingredients we can. All our ingredients are certified organic.

Omnivore Salt is versatile: use it as a rub for grilling or roasting, as a foundation for sauces, soups, sosu-vide recipes and dressings. Sprinkle it on eggs and vegetables.

Product description

Omnivore Salt, is a delightful blend of natural sea salt, harvested from the salt beds off the coast of Northern California, and organic spices. It's great as a rub on meat and fish, on vegetables, in soups & stews, sprinkled on top of eggs, or in a salad dressing. It will enhance the natural flavor of your food and create a delicate and unique experience for the palate.
