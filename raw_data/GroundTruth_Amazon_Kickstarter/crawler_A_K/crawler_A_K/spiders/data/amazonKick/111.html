Product description

Santa Barbara Ranch is a tangy ranch flavored green pea snack. Our Santa Barbara Ranch flavored peas have that tangy, sour cream and herb taste popularized at a dude ranch outside of Santa Barbara, California. Made in a plant that processes peanuts. Suitable for vegans. Produced in a BRC certified and OU Kosher facility in Suzhou, China made from all non-GMO ingredients.
