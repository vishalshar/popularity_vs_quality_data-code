Product Description

Switchmate automatically turns on lights as you arrive home or enter rooms and is installed through a simple one-second installation, so everyone can enjoy the many benefits of smart lighting without the hassle of complicated set ups. It is the first smart home product that can be use with absolutely no configuration, tools, wiring, or wifi. Switchmate instantly attaches magnetically to existing toggle or rocker switches and is controlled through the free app on your smart phone. Always makes it look like you are home for enhanced security with Switchmates timers that automatically turn your lights on/off, and function even when you’re away from your home. Switchmate instantly modernizes old, outdated light switches and transform in-home décor, which can be conveniently moved around the home or taken when you relocate. Just snap, tap, and start enjoying smart lighting simplified with Switchmate.

4.0 out of 5 starsTrouble with Metal Wall Plates
Byragushon March 31, 2017
Color: White|Verified Purchase
I bought 3 Switchmates. All three had the same issue. I inserted the batteries and the mechanism went up and down when I pressed the front button. Then I placed it over the toggle switch and when I pressed the front button, nothing happened. I tried adjusting the switch placement. It worked a few times, but then stopped working. When I removed it from the wall switch and pressed the front button, it worked beginning with the 2nd press.

Turns out that the problem was caused by the wall plates: they are metal. The magnets on the back are attracted to both the screws and the wall plate, likely setting up a magnetic field that interferes with the electronics inside the Switchmate. I replaced the wall plates with plastic ones, and all 3 now work flawlessly. I have a Wink 2 hub and an Amazon Echo, so I can turn them on and off with a voice command, e.g., "Alexa, turn on the kitchen lights."

I would give them 5 stars if somewhere in the directions it said to avoid metal wall plates. That would have saved me a lot of time.

Bottom Line: the Switchmates have problems with metal wallplates, use nonmetal ones.
