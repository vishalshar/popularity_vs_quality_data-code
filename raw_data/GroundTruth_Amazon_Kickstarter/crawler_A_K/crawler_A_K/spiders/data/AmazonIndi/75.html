Product description

Product Description

HÜD (rhymes with ""mood"") is Swedish for skin. It is the latest and greatest tool in the fight against skin cancer. HÜD is a medical-grade lens that attaches to virtually any smartphone, allowing you to take crystal-clear images of the moles on your skin. It includes a slide-on case that fits on any iPhone 6 or 6S, plus a universal mount compatible with any smartphone up to 3.1"" (78mm) in width. Conveniently, HÜD may be recharged by plugging it right into any standard USB outlet. Once you receive your HÜD, download the First Derm companion app, available on iOS or Android. The app will educate you on the warning signs of skin cancer so you’ll know what to look out for. The app also stores a detailed archive of your HÜD images. When you want a spot evaluated, send it in to First Derm’s network of board-certified dermatologists for review. The dermatologist will let you know if the mole requires an immediate biopsy, or if it’s in the clear until your next regular checkup.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Simple, convenient, medical-grade.

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

The idea behind HÜD was not thought up overnight or in the garage - it's the result of nearly 7 years of research, user testing and validation by First Derm and DermLite. With our expertise in mobile dermatology, we knew there had to be a way for us to address the thousands of preventable skin cancer deaths per year. Fortunately, there is already a great tool for early detection of skin cancer called a dermatoscope. A dermatoscope is a specialized lens that helps a dermatologist tell if a mole is cancerous or benign. Unfortunately, they're not made for consumer use or budgets. Since 2014, we've worked on HÜD with the goal of making dermatoscopes mobile, user-friendly, and affordable.

What makes your product special?

HÜD is the only FDA-registered medical-grade skin scope - developed by the leading brand of portable skin scopes for dermatologists - that has been designed for everyone with a smartphone. HÜD simply works: Through our various public health campaigns, we’ve screened thousands of moles and detected numerous deadly melanomas which might have otherwise gone unnoticed.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

Since up to 95% of skin cancer deaths are preventable if they're detected early, we get excited every time our product and technology is instrumental in discovering potentially deadly skin disease.
