Product description

Product Description

Introducing the first Bluetooth MIDI interface that universally supports most MIDI controllers. Finally, your favorite Class-Compliant USB-MIDI or 5-pin MIDI controllers can wirelessly connect to apps and DAW’s on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac in an easy-to-use device. Powered by two AA batteries and utilizing Apple’s Bluetooth MIDI connectivity, the puc+ gives wireless freedom and convenience to all of your MIDI controllers with very low latency. If you’re a musician with an iPhone or iPad loaded with GarageBand and some amazing synth apps, but connecting your favorite controller with adapters and specialty cables makes you feel like you’re tethering up a 21st Century ball and chain, the puc+ is the wireless solution you’ve been waiting for. Attach your keyboard controller to a puc+, your drum pad controller to another puc+, your fader bank controller to another, and your trademark MIDI synth module to yet another and they’re all wireless and available in your DAW or from apps on your iPad or iPhone. Apple’s Bluetooth MIDI connectivity also supports MULTIPLE simultaneous connections. Put a puc+ on each of your favorite controllers and connect them all at the same time. (iOS 8+, OS X 10.10+ required). If you love your wires because you believe they perform best, you owe it to yourself to try the puc+’s low latency.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Wireless. Fast. MIDI.

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

There are hundreds of great synthesizer and music creation apps available in the iTunes App Store, but making music using touchscreens can leave your performances feeling a little flat. You’d like to use your existing MIDI controllers with your iPad or iPhone, but the specialty adapters and cables aren’t convenient or reliable. Zivix wanted to connect MIDI to iOS and OS X devices wirelessly, and the puc+ does just that; makes you MIDI controllers wireless! And now that Android 6.0 supports Bluetooth MIDI connections your Android gear can also support your existing MIDI controllers.

What makes your product special?

You have MIDI controllers you love. It would be a shame to toss your favorite controllers aside just to support a new controller with wireless Bluetooth support, especially if you don’t like how the controller plays or feels. The puc+ makes your EXISTING controllers wireless - no need to replace your favorites! If you need to route MIDI from your iOS or OS X device to a piece of MIDI hardware the puc+ can serve as a wireless MIDI Out for your favorite software.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

When the original puc was in development, Apple’s Bluetooth MIDI Connectivity didn’t exist and Bluetooth had questionable “real time” delivery of performance data, so Zivix wrote and developed our own wireless MIDI protocol for WiFi. While it worked well, the user experience was more complicated than you’d like it to be. When Apple announced Bluetooth MIDI to developers we knew we could move our existing code quickly, and what happens behind the scenes in iOS and OS X makes the new puc+ an even better wireless solution.
