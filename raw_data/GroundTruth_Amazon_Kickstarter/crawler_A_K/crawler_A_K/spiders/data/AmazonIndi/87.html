Product description

Color:Light Wood

Airsense is a smart air monitor and purifier. Equipped with the top of the line air sensors, it will inform you about the air quality, temperature, and humidity in your baby's room. The monitor will alert you about volatile organic compounds (VOC), such as carbon monoxide, methane, ammonia, and others ones that might be in the air. It will also send you lifestyle Suggestions to improve the wellbeing of your family. Air quality indicator lights will change the color if the air quality changes. You will be able to pick custom colors you prefer for the lights. The monitor also features two-way speakers, alarm, and air station map to track nearby air sense monitors. Airsense comes with a free iBaby care app (iOS and Android compatible) to control the monitor.

4.0 out of 5 starsExcellent, but with one very important flaw
ByLisa Dalyon December 29, 2016
Color: Dark Wood
This is a really cool device with an important flaw that you may need to know about. I received one as a gift and had difficulty setting it up at home. The install process kept failing with no message about why. It has to be connected to your internet in order to work, but despite entering the correct password in the app several times, it kept failing to connect. After some online research, we determined that it cannot successfully connect if your wifi password contains anything other than letters or numbers AND the password string can't be too long. So, we went to great pains with Time Warner to get our wifi password changed to something shorter and more conventional (and, I might add, less safe!) After doing this, success! It works and offers fascinating data on the indoor air quality, and also works as an air purifier.

My actual intention was to use this in the office, but guess what? No self respecting office wifi has a password that will work with this, and it is essentially a pretty, light-up brick if not connected to wifi. So, I will happilly use mine at home instead of the office, but wanted others to know that if this will be an issue, probably should look for another product.
