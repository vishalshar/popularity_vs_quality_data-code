About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Fall In Love

Where did you get the inspiration for your product?

I read an article in the New York Times about Dr. Arthor Aron’s Research into helping couples fall in love. I was so inspired I spent the entire day creating an app to present the questions in an easy to use way. I launched the app that day, and by morning over 300,000 people had checked it out, including Mark Zuckerberg sharing it on Facebook! Motivated by the amazing response I wanted to create a more intimate and sacred experience by creating a physical card game. The initial crowdfunding campaign was a huge success, raising over 300% of it’s initial goal in 30 days.

What differentiates your product from similar products out there?

The Love Game is based on scientific research that has been responsible for thousands of people falling in love! The Love Game was the original card game designed to help people fall in love and we’ve been featured in tons of press from Forbes to NBC. The Love Game also comes with a variety of bonus questions from our curated group of Love Leaders including New York Times Bestselling Author Marci Shimoff.

Tell us about the best and most challenging parts of the creation process.

The Creative process is at it’s a core, a spiritual one. Confronting fears of failure and solving problems and trusting things will work out when you have no idea if they will.. This is all deeply challenging and rewarding.
