About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Relieve your memories

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

The biggest inspiration behind this was my family. I am a happily married man with 2 kids. Smartphone have made it easier me to capture those moments that mean the most to us, from birthday parties, to my daughter's first steps. We sit back and watch past videos on our TVs and phones which is great, but that's where it stops. And like every CEO does, I want more. Why not relive those moments and get that feeling back you had the day it happened? I want to bring that special feeling back which normal video hardly can. I want you to feel like you teleported back in time and to truly immerse yourself in the situation whenever you want. With VR Technology, that can be done. Lately, Virtual Reality has been booming. There are plenty of research, ideas about VR can change how we think, how we enjoy the worlds around us. I partially feel inspire with Chris Milk's Empathy Machine base on Immersive VR. Chris' ideas was that feelings can actually be teleported through space. How about through time. Those ideas simmers in my head for months. And now, my team and I proudly share the Teleport VR with the world. It was so amazing to see my little boy thrill when he took his first swim again.

What makes your product special?

This is the first Virtual Reality 3D Camera that have ever been made for daily usage. It is small, affordable and so easy to install that you can bring it around and ready to shoot.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

Nothing can beats awesome feedbacks and the Teleport app keep filling up with heartwarming moments
