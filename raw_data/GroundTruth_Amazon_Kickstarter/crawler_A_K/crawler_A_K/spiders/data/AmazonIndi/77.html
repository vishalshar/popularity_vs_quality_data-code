Product description

Product Description

QLIPP is the ultimate tennis performance sensor. It measures every part of your stroke, analyzing the spin, speed and sweet spot accuracy of each shot-- designed to improve your game with the most advanced analytics possible.

From the Manufacturer

How it works

The QLIPP sensor tracking technology attaches to your tennis racket quickly and begins recording your swings and movement right away. During practice, it transfers all the data to the QLIPP mobile app in real-time so you can get immediate insights on your strengths, weaknesses, and how to improve your game. It's like having your own digital tennis instructor helping you get better all the time!

Fits any racquet

With our registered twist and lock design, QLIPP takes less time to setup than lacing your shoes. Collect, analyze and store your data easily. At 8g, it will be your lightest and most important piece of gear.

Analyze Every Stroke

Push past your limits and create new personal records. Record and evaluate all your strokes based on stamina, consistency and heaviness. Unleash your fullest potential by refining your techniques, harnessing greater power and hitting more sweet spots than ever before.

View your Video Playback

Become a better player by knowing yourself better. See yourself from a new perspective through our video playback so that you will always be better than before. With slow-motion playback, you are now able to correct your posture to create that perfect stroke. You can now even easily share your videos along with all its data anywhere you want, anytime.

Compare with Your Performance

The QLIPP App also connects to your social networks, letting you share your progress on your pages, organize matches with local players and even show you how your stats compare on the QLIPP community leaderboards. You can compete and track your progress in our community in an effort to keep motivated to improve your game.

QLIPP App

The QLIPP app can save and monitor each session so you can track your improvement and see what parts of your game you need to work on. From utilizing your smartphone’s camera for video analysis of your form to providing session by session progress tracking, the app can help you identify errors in your form and provide guidelines on how to develop a stronger swing.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Lightest tennis sensor

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

We started with a vision to make QLIPP something that could help all tennis players play better. What you see today is the result of more than two years of Research and Development. A lot of effort, resources and promises have been poured into this and we are truly happy and excited to be able to have the opportunity to present to you and the tennis community, the QLIPP sensor.

What makes your product special?

Apart from being one of the lightest in the market, the QLIPP Ultimate Tennis Performance Sensor can be attached to any racquet with its registered "twist and lock” technology.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

We started pre-production as early as Feb and have been sending out beta samples to users. Seeing these beta users fall in love with the product have encouraged us that our effforts do pay off and incentivize us to do even better
