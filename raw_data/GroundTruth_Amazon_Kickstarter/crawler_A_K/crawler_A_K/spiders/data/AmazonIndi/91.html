Product description

Meet Cocoon, the simplest way to protect your whole home. Weve designed everything you need to secure your home into a single, smart device that uses a unique sound-based AI to cover and protect your whole home. Cocoon keeps you and your family feeling safe without the need for complicated or expensive installation, or the hassle of monthly fees. Cocoons unique and powerful Subsound technology means your whole home can be secured with just one device the size of a baseball. Each device is beautifully engineered, containing a HD Camera, Night Vision, Motion Detection as well as a Very Loud Siren. Cocoon learns and adapts to your home, automatically arming when you leave home and disarming when you come home. The patented Subsound technology quickly learns to avoid false alarms, sending you smart notifications via our free apps for Android and iPhone, so you can take action wherever you may be. Cocoon is the future of home security. Just turn it on and relax.

Really 3.5 / 5. It does alert me when there are significant noise events at home while I'm gone (i.e. the dog has a barking fit, someone opens the garage door, etc.) I'm not fully sold on the "sub sound" technology, since the trigger noises are significant enough that a standard security camera with a mic would probably pick them up. I tried a few tests where I opened other exterior doors and windows while the system was armed and it didn't trigger any alerts. Maybe this will be improved thru software updates, but as is, I don't trust this system to detect break-ins before the intruder has gotten inside the house. Also, for a fully wired camera, the streaming quality of the video seems pretty low, with significant buffering and delay. Also, a few times I got alerts because the internal mic seemed to cut out completely for a few seconds. Perhaps this is due to the device over-heating?
