Product Description

If you can write with a pen, you can draw in 3D! CreoPop is the first, wireless 3D-printing pen with cool inks. Create 3D art pieces by hand utilizing integrated LEDs that solidify the ink as you draw, while keeping the pen and 3D art piece cool to the touch. Easily change out ink colors while you draw to create unique, multicolored 3D art pieces.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Cordless 3D-Printing Pen

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

I’d tested out other 3D printing pens and burned my finger tip, I knew that day their had to be better solution and started working with my team to develop a product that was cordless and cool to the touch.

What makes your product special?

Our technology is simple, safe and cordless. Using proprietary photopolymers our creation medium cures under LED lights, rather than the conventional PLA melted plastic approach. By using our photopolymers our product and creations remain cool-to-the-touch and, as we aren’t melting anything, we use significantly less power allowing CreoPop to be completely cordless.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

Our Indiegogo campaign was thrilling, but just the beginning of a long journey to scale the technology. While the CreoPop 3D printing pen is the first consumer ready pen, we went through countless design iterations before we felt we had the pen mastered. It was a grueling process but to see the finished piece in the hands of our backers, and new consumer who saw the product in-store, has been by-far the most rewarding part of the journey.
