Product Description

Over the years, looking for keys, phones, wallet, and personal items has always being a time consuming issue. The time spent by an average person annually is close to twelve days. Thanks to our advancement in technology, we have made a great solution that makes your things more secure and saves you time.

MYNT is a tiny fob that creates freedom from looking for things. It also provides control over things remotely. MYNT is the world’s thinnest tracker with advanced remote control functions. It is easily attachable to wallets, keys, and any personal time in various sizes. Once attached, MYNT will track the location of the item within Bluetooth range. Smart alarms will be generated once the tracker is out of range.

It is now possible to take control of your time and take remote control of Bluetooth connectable devices. MYNT is your companion for better management of things in your life.

Phone-Finder

People spend valuable time on looking for personal things. One tiny remote that can remote control many devices can be helpful in many situations. Here comes MYNT.

4.0 out of 5 starsGreat Device with a Few Partner App Hiccups
ByJohn Victor Ubanaon July 2, 2016
Color: Black|Verified Purchase
I’ve purchased this item with a discounted price in exchange for my honest review.

The Package:
The product arrived in a really cute little box. Even though it is small, all you need to make the device work is inside the package; inside you will find the MYNT device, an extra battery, a keychain tool to hook it with an item such a bag or keys, and 2 double sided tapes in case you intended to stick it to an item that you wanted to track.

The MYNT Device:
The device is super light, thin, and looks great too – I’ve attached it with my keys and it fits perfectly. On one of the sides you will find a latch that you can use to change the battery. I seemed to find difficulty to unlatch it with my fingers and nails alone, maybe because my nails are not that long. Using a tip of something sharp like a small scissors I was able to take out the battery. You don’t have to do this often, which is great. It only comes with one button, and with the app that you need to download you can configure what the button does (e.g. camera clicker). I had fun testing the ring function if I misplace my phone, by holding on to the button for about 3 seconds your paired device will ring. You can also find MYNT if you have your phone and you’ve lost MYNT. In app you can press on the ring function and the MYNT device will beep, it’s not as loud but you can distinguish it easily.

MYNT App and Pairing:
After downloading the App from Google Play I’ve started the pairing function; at first I have trouble with the pairing process, my Note5 running 6.0 (Marshmallow) was able to find MYNT in the Bluetooth settings, the app however failed to do so. Here is what I did:
1. Remove the battery from the MYTN and put it back in.
2. Restart my phone
3. Reinstall the app
After doing the above troubleshooting steps, the MYTN app finally found the MYNT device.

The App is user friendly and is easy to understand. The simple interface will make it easy for you to find MYTN and the item you’re tracking with ease; this is also true vice versa when you’ve lost MYTN and you have your phone instead. Also during my test, the app will warn you via Notification that you’re away from your Tracked item.

Other Issues:
After the first pairing I was warned that my device needs a new firmware update, I’ve pressed on proceed and it downloaded the new firmware. During the installation, the application told me that it failed and I’ve pressed on try again, and it seems that the app is not doing anything for about 10 minutes and so I’ve stopped the update. Then I’ve closed and relaunched the app, after doing that the firmware update notification is gone – therefore I am unsure if I’ve installed the new firmware or not. I can’t also find a setting or option in the app to check on firmware or firmware updates.

Overall the MYNT tracking device is great and I would definitely buying it again to track other items or even my pets (or my wife… LOL).
