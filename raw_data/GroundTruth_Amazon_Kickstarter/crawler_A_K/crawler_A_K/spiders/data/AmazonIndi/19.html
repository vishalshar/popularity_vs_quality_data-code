Product Description

Starting solids is one of the major transitions of your baby's first year. Ergonomically designed by MIT graduates and devoted parents, Spuni’s patented tulip design triggers baby’s natural latching instinct to help baby transition more naturally to solids. Spuni's innovative design also prevents baby from choking on too much food, making mealtimes safer and a lot less messy. Spuni baby spoons are just the right length to reach the bottom of food jars and bowls. Made in Germany from food safe, medical grade TPU – the same material used in pacifiers and baby bottle nipples. BPA free, BPC free and Phthalates and PVC free. Each set includes a clear plastic travel case with two baby spoons in bright attractive colors that babies love. Hypoallergenic and dishwasher safe. Ideal for 4+ month babies. Before Spuni, infant spoons were just miniaturized versions of adult spoons, designed with little regard for an infant’s needs. Spuni first baby spoon has been designed through countless hours of observation and it's unique patented tulip design makes starting solids safer, easier and less messy for babies and parents everywhere!

Spuni - Less Messy, More Spuni

Spuni's mission is to support early development in children. Baby's first spoon by Spuni was designed by two MIT graduates to help their children transition to solids. Their children loved it, their friends' children loved it and the rest is history. Our office is located in the state-of-the-art New Lab facility in Brooklyn. We invite you to come visit us if you're in the neighborhood!

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Babys first spoon

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

We have kids and realized that existing spoons were unsafe and didn't help a baby transition from nursing or bottle feeding to solids, leading to a frustrated and messy baby (and parents!).

What makes your product special?

The Spuni spoon has been build from the ground up by using baby's natural latching instinct to make it easier for a transition to solid foods. It meets a baby's developmental needs at that age better than other first spoons, leading to a happier, less messy baby.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

Using our own products on our own family – because it's just better than anything out there.
