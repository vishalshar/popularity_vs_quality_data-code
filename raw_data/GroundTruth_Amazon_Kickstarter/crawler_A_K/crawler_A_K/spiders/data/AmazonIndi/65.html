Product Description

FOBO Tire Plus is the World’s First all Bluetooth Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring System. It is designed to promote road safety through the only contact points of vehicles to the road surface – the tires. Optimum tire pressure ensures optimal road holding and car performance, effective braking distance, longer tire tread life and better fuel efficiency. FOBO brand is an acronym for For Our Better wOrld. Our vision is to design products that embrace new technologies to enhance human living and preserve the environment.

From the Manufacturer

The air is free, don’t drive without it!

The most critical contact points of a moving vehicle to the road surface are the rolling tires. The tires need proper pressure to perform optimally. Low tire pressure causes fuel inefficiency, reduced car performance, poor handling, and puncture or blow-out when driving. Statistics shows that tires underinflated by more than 25 percent are three times more likely to be involved in a crash related to tire problems. FOBO Tire makes the monitoring of tire pressure handily and smartly to provide you with peace of mind and confidence in tire performance.

Best of science, best of design

FOBO Tire gives accurate and real-time reading of tire pressures when you are in Bluetooth range, 24/7. It protects you and passengers from the danger of inconspicuously underinflated tires and helps save costs with fuel efficiency and prolonged tire life. Designed with DIY installation, it can serve many years with user replaceable battery.


24/7 Monitoring

FOBO Tire monitors tire pressure and temperature even when the car ignition is off. One can get an instant real time update when in Bluetooth range.


Multiple Cars Monitoring

One can monitor within one FOBO Tire App up to 19 sets of separately installed FOBO Tire. This enables easy monitoring of multiple cars.


FOBOShare

FOBO Tire App allows up to 100 authorized users to receive readings from the same FOBO Tire. This quick sharing and intuitive feature enable multiple drivers who share one car to enjoy the features of FOBO Tire on their very own phones.


Easy DIY with 2-level Theft Deterrent Feature

Install FOBO Tire in less than 5 minutes without the need for professional help. Just screw on the sensor and follow the App's on-screen instructions. There is a 2–level theft deterrent feature, namely a mechanical lock nut and a cloud-based ID lock. The theft deterrent lock nut requires a proprietary wrench to unscrew. A cloud ID locks the sensors and In-car unit to owner’s FOBO Cloud account rendering stolen sensors and In-Car unit not re-usable. You will also be notified by the App if any tire sensor is missing.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Simply Smart Monitoring

Where did you get the inspiration for your product?

We felt that tires being the only contact points between the road surface and fast moving car are very critical in road safety and have to be at optimal performance at all times. Given that tire air pressure is a critical factor for optimal tire performance and that the air is actually free, we felt strongly to create a device that is smart and easy to use to help in monitoring tire pressure.

What differentiates your product from similar products out there?

24/7 Monitoring using a smart phone, Multiple cars monitoring and multiple driver sharing.

Tell us about the best and most challenging parts of the creation process.

It is very challenging to get a smart phone to read real time pressure when the car ignition is switched off and to enable a user to recover all settings with a simple email sign in when he loses his phone. It is also very challenging to allow 100 drivers to read from the same set of FOBO Tire.

Front & Rear = 30 PSI
