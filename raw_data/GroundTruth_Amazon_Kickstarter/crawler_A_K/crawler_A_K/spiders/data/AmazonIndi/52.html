Product Description

Ultra-soft antimicrobial PICC line cover designed to help keep your PICC line in place. The unique mesh window provides breathability for the PICC line and dressing and visibility for you and your caretaker. It is machine washable and re-usable. Designed to help you be a person first, patient second. Visit www.careandwear.com for more information.

Designed With You In Mind

When you are receiving treatment, the last thing you want is another reminder of what you're going through. And that’s why we've created a people-focused, problem-focused solution that is made for your needs. It is designed to make you feel like yourself.

Let's Get Back to Living

We want you to undergo your treatment, your way. The soft anti-microbial sleeve allows you to wear your style on your sleeve. It comes in a variety of styles and designs. Get back to being you.


Antimicrobial Fabric

The soft, antimicrobial fabric is comfortable and easy to wear


Breathable

The unique design allows for airflow and visibility


Machine Washable

Machine wash and dry with your other clothes


Our Science, Your Style

The Care+Wear PICC Line Cover is an arm band that is as functional as it is fashionable. Not only does it allow for the breathability that the PICC line dressing requires, but it’s also made with a flexible, antimicrobial fabric that offers the mobility, discretion and the peace of mind that you need to do the everyday things that make you feel more like yourself.

About the Startup

Describe your product in 3 words.

Simple, soft, secure

How did you come up with the idea for this product?

We, like so many others, had friends and family members diagnosed and receiving treatment through a PICC line. To protect their PICC line site, they were told to wear a tube sock, which made an uncomfortable treatment even more awkward. This bulky tube sock became a constant reminder of their illness and their uphill battle towards recovery. We decided to create something new, something better, that would help patients living with PICC lines see themselves as people first, and patients second.

What makes your product special?

We wanted to create a product that nurses and doctors would want their patients to wear. And so we partnered with leading hospitals to develop an arm band that has a window for breathability that the PICC line dressing requires and is also made with a comfortable, antimicrobial fabric.

What has been the best part of your startup experience?

We knew so little about PICC lines before Care+Wear. We’ve since learned that over 6 million PICC lines are prescribed each year to the nearly 14 million people diagnosed with cancer each year, in addition to the 300,000 individuals who are living with PICCs as a result of Lyme disease and a number of other type of conditions. Now, more than ever, we are motivated to find creative solutions and develop innovative products that make people feel more like themselves that we’ve dubbed the “healthwear” industry.
